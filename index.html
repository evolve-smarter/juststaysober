Who Built This

Built by someone who takes clients to meetings every week.

This app started from a real frustration: watching clients in early recovery struggle to find the right meeting. Not just any meeting — the right one. The kind that fits where they are, what they believe, and who they are.

Someone coming off opioids might need NA on Monday and a SMART meeting on Thursday. Someone with a family member struggling needs Al-Anon — but the AA app they downloaded doesn't show it. Someone leaving treatment needs an IOP — but there's no app that shows IOPs, meetings, and sober livings together.

Every week, I watch people use Google, phone trees, and word-of-mouth to piece together resources that should just exist in one place. That's why Just Stay Sober is being built.

"Recovery is hard enough. Finding a meeting shouldn't be."
